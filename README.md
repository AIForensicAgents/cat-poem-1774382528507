## 📜 The Poem

> The Alchemy of Whiskers
> 
> They knead the morning into sunshine pats,
> soft as saucers, heavy with secret spells.
> Whiskers map the air like silver compass arcs,
> and every blink is a benediction of trust.
> 
> Tail a curving wand that sketches mischief,
> they leap through housebeams, small comets at play.
> Mouse-signals fade beneath velvet paws,
> while carpets remember each royal parade.
> 
> Purrs are tiny engines of repair and grace,
> a low tide of comfort that hushes hard edges.
> Eyes hold starlight folded into private rooms,
> where moonbeams come to nap on sleeping things.
> 
> They fold themselves into laps like folded maps,
> teach us how to dwell in warmth and hush.
> When night loosens its pockets of wonder,
> cats keep the keys and lend us their small magic.
